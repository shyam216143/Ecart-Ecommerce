    Product Description
    Super AMOLED Screen

    Redmi Note 10 Pro boasts a 16.9 cm (6.67) 120 Hz Super AMOLED Display that enables you to appreciate and experience vivid and authentic colours that appear to come alive. Furthermore, the large touchscreen of this phone offers an excellent visual experience, taking your fun factor to a different level.

    Terrific Gaming Experience

    With an incredible 120 Hz refresh rate, this smartphone provides a terrific gaming experience. Furthermore, the characters from your favourite game are brought to life, allowing you to immerse yourself in gaming brilliance.

    Ultra Bright Display

    With a peak brightness of 1200 nits, the display of this phone offers exceptionally bright vistas. Furthermore, it is convenient to use even in scorching sunlight and offers a great picture quality.

    Colour-rich Visuals

    The device features a DCI-P3 broad colour gamut of 100 %, providing a look and feel that is as close to reality as possible. Additionally, the DCI-P3 enhances the colours, giving you a vibrant experience.

    Robust Corning Gorilla Glass 5

    Redmi Note 10 Pro sports a Corning Gorilla Glass 5 that safeguards your display from inadvertent damages and falls. This way, you can take this phone anywhere with you on trekking adventures.
